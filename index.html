Pregnancy Helper

Pregnancy Helper is a web application that provides assistance for pregnant women. It provides the information of monthly progress of their baby and the nutrients that should be taken care of.

Tools used: MongoDB, Node.js, HTML and CSS.

Pregnancy Helper

It also provides a platform for pregnancy women to share their pregnancy stories and their experience during the period. This web app was created in a team of three at HackFest Kathmandu 2019 and we were selected as the 2nd Runner up of the event.

Krisi Doctor

Krisi Doctor  is a mobile app which is used to classify the diseases in plants with the help of the patterns that are  observed in the leaves. It identifies 35 diseases in plants. The entire app is targeted for Nepali farmers.

Krisi Doctor

It classifies the images based on image classification using CNN algorithm.

Tools used: React Native, Tensorflow, Node.js and MongoDB.

This was created in a team of three. It is the academic major project under Kathmandu Engineering College.
